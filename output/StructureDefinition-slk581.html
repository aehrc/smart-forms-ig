Statistical linkage key 581 represented as an identifier datatype. Specification for generating the SLK581 is definied by the Australian Institute of Health and Welfare, https://meteor.aihw.gov.au/content/750410

Usages:

  • This DataType Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide
