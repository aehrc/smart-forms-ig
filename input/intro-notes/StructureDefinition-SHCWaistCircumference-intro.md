### Usage Scenarios
The following are supported usage scenarios for this profile:
* Search for a patient's waist circumference information for the purposes of prepopulating the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Record a patient's waist circumference information in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Save a patient's waist circumference information that were recorded in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form to the patient's record. This includes newly recorded waist circumference only.
