### Usage Scenarios
The following are supported usage scenarios for this profile:
* Search for a patient's problem list conditions for the purposes of prepopulating the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Record a patient's problem list conditions in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Save a patient's problem list conditions that were recorded in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form to the patient's record. This includes newly recorded conditions only. Updates to existing conditions are supported by the [Smart Health Checks Patch Condition](StructureDefinition-SHCPatchCondition.html) profile.
