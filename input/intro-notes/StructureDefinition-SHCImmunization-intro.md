### Usage Scenarios
The following are supported usage scenarios for this profile:
* Search for a patient's immunisations for the purposes of prepopulating the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Record a patient's immunisations in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Save a patient's immunisations that were recorded in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form to the patient's record. This includes newly recorded immunisations only.
