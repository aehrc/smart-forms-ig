### Usage Scenarios
The following are supported usage scenarios for this profile:
* Search for a patient's medication use statements for the purposes of prepopulating the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Record a patient's medication use statements in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Save a patient's medication use statements that were recorded in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form to the patient's record. This includes newly recorded medications only. Updates to existing medications are supported by the [Smart Health Checks Patch MedicationStatement](StructureDefinition-SHCPatchMedicationStatement.html) profile.
