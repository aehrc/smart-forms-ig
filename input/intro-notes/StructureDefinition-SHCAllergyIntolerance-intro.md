### Usage Scenarios
The following are supported usage scenarios for this profile:
* Search for a patient's allergies/adverse reactions for the purposes of prepopulating the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Record a patient's allergies/adverse reactions in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form
* Save a patient's allergies/adverse reactions that were recorded in the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Check form to the patient's record. This includes newly recorded allergies/adverse reactions only. Updates to existing allergies/adverse reactions are supported by the [Smart Health Checks Patch AllergyIntolerance](StructureDefinition-SHCPatchAllergyIntolerance.html) profile.
